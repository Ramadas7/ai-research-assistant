AI Research Assistant

A local-first, privacy-respecting RAG pipeline for reasoning over research documents.

Pipeline

PDF → text/table/OCR/vision extraction → chunking → embeddings → ChromaDB → semantic retrieval → Llama 3.2 → cited answer.

Multi-document

Every chunk is tagged with its source document, so you can query across several PDFs at once or ask for a direct comparison.

Figures & scanned pages

Scanned pages are OCR'd with Tesseract. Embedded figures and graphs are described by a vision model (llama3.2-vision) so they become searchable text.

Memory

Conversations are saved per session in SQLite, so follow-up questions and revisited chats keep full context.

100% local

Embeddings and generation both run on-device via Ollama — no document content ever leaves your machine.

Stack

Flask · ChromaDB · sentence-transformers · pdfplumber · PyMuPDF · Tesseract · Ollama (Llama 3.2 + Llama 3.2 Vision)
